Brecon White is a code junkie, design nut and game fanatic. With a degree in Interactive Multimedia at Carleton University, he has a wide knowledge of all things design, development and media. Though he began in graphic design, illustration and artistic endeavours, he has picked up a great love of code. Nothing brings him more joy than seeing a program, or website, compile correctly.

In his spare time, he works at creating independant games and game engines, with a focus on stories that will hopefully inspire others to keep creating and exploring their own worlds.
